Grilled Chocolate Sandwich

Description

This is JUST like making a grilled cheese, but you are using chocolate! Created this while craving chocolate and carbs.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon softened butter, divided
  • 2 (1/2 inch thick) slices soft French bread
  • 2 tablespoons semi-sweet chocolate chips, or more to taste
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • Â¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over medium-low heat. Spread half the butter on one piece of French bread; place butter-side down into warm skillet. Sprinkle bread with chocolate chips; cook until chocolate begins to melt and bread is lightly browned, 2 to 3 minutes.

  2. Spread remaining butter on second piece of bread. Place bread butter-side up on top of melted chocolate. Flip the sandwich and cook until bread is browned on other side, 2 to 3 minutes. Remove from heat; sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon.
